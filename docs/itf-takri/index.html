ITF Takri

May 20, 2021 1 min read

ITF Takri typeface was created by Rahul Gajjar as part of graduation project at National Institute of Design. This project was sponsored by the Indian Type Foundry. ITF Takri is a companion typeface for ITF Devanagari typeface.
