Takri Letterforms

May 20, 2021 1 min read

On one axis there is a list of all of the Takri characters; consonants, vowels, conjuncts, numbers, diacritics, and other signs. And the second axis is the shape of the characters found in the manuscripts and other resources from oldest to newest. This would help to get a sense of how characters looked like during different centuries.

These are the books referenced for making the table of Takri letterforms evolution. These books contain a table of different script, the letterforms from different sources are also added.

After analyzing the table you could see two major sets for Takri alphabets. The old version of Takri and the modern form of Takri. The older version mainly appears in the old manuscript samples and the modern version is majorly visible in the latest samples found of Takri like handwritten notes, land records, printed samples.

Based on the table, this raw skeleton was created which has been taken as base for Takri typeface.
